Payment Options

Our fee for service is $60.00 an hour and any additional time is charged accordingly. We do not file insurance but are happy to provide a claim form with necessary information for out-of-network re-imbursement.

We have several churches that we partner with that underwrite some of the counseling fees for their members. Please contact us to discuss the various options.
