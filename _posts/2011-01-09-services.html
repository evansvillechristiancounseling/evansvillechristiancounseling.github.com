Payment Options

Our fee for service is $60.00 an hour and any additional time is charged accordingly at a rate of $1/minute. We do not file insurance but are happy to provide a claim form with necessary information for out-of-network re-imbursement.

We have several churches that we partner with that underwrite some of the counseling fees for their members. Please contact us to discuss the various options.

We do accept cash, check, and most major credit cards.
