Counselor - Ellie Malek

Ellie Malek grew up on Evansville’s west side. She received a Bachelor’s in Counseling at Southwestern Assemblies of God University in Waxahachie, Texas. Later she earned a Master’s in Social Work at the University of Southern Indiana.

Ellie is licensed with the State of Indiana as a Licensed Social Worker. She has been in full-time ministry with her husband for over five years. She works at St Mary’s Medical Center as a social worker and teaches at Ivy Tech Community College. She has counseled those with severe mental illness for the State of Oklahoma’s Department of Mental Health and has also worked in adoption.

Seeking wisdom from the ultimate Counselor, Ellie knows God has a plan and purpose for every human being. Because of this, she believes there is hope for every person.

Ellie’s goal in session is for her clients to feel heard, understood, and to create a collaborative plan of action to pursue. Ellie knows that with God’s help all things are possible!
